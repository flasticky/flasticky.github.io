About me

My introduction

I acquired my first experiences in high school, where I specialized in computer network administration. Then I continued my studies at the Faculty of Information Technology at the Czech Technical University (CTU) focusing on Information Security. During my studies, I gained experience in e-commerce at Alza, which gave me a new perspective on the dynamics of the business environment. I also devoted time to assembling and servicing computers, which provided me with practical skills and a better understanding of user needs. Furthermore, I expanded my education on platforms such as TryHackMe (currently in top 2%) and PortSwigger Academy, where I developed my skills in cybersecurity.
